About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of scholars and Imams, graduates of Al-Azhar Al-Sharif University, the oldest Islamic university in the world. We hold a higher degree (Bachelor's) in distinguished Sharia specializations, including:

Fundamentals of Religion (Usul al-Din)

Study of creed, exegesis (Tafsir), and Hadith.

Islamic Sharia

Study of Fiqh (jurisprudence), its principles (Usul al-Fiqh), and comparative law.

We carry the message of knowledge and Da'wah (call to Islam), and strive to spread a moderate, enlightened understanding of Islam, based on profound knowledge and the moderate Azhari methodology.

We work to provide reliable religious content, based on authentic sources, in a contemporary style that meets the needs of Muslims in their time, without negligence or extremism.
