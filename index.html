About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of scholars and Imams, graduates of Al-Azhar Al-Sharif University. We hold degrees in:

Usul al-Din

Study of creed, exegesis (Tafsir), and Hadith.

Islamic Sharia

Study of Fiqh, its principles, and comparative law.

We carry the message of knowledge and Da'wah, striving to spread a moderate, enlightened understanding of Islam.
