About Us

Who We Are

We are a group of scholars and Imams, graduates of Al-Azhar Al-Sharif University, holding higher degrees in Sharia specializations (Fundamentals of Religion, Sharia and Law, Arabic Language).

We strive to spread a moderate understanding of Islam, serving the Muslim community in London through fatwas, consultations, and family guidance, all within the framework of UK law.

Our Vision

To be a pioneer in providing Shariah services that promote family stability and social cohesion.
